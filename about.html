What is this tool?

The Average Grade Calculator is designed to help students easily keep track of their academic performance. This tool allows users to input various types of assessments such as exams, quizzes, pop quizzes, midterms, and projects. By entering the scores for each of these items, students can get a comprehensive overview of their grades and understand how they are performing across different assignments.

Using the calculator is straightforward. Users simply select an item from the provided list and input its corresponding score, which should be between 1 and 100. Upon pressing the 'Calculate' button, the app generates a detailed table that displays each item, the respective score, and the letter grade associated with that score. The grading scale ranges from A++ to F, providing clear and precise feedback on each entered score.

Additionally, the Average Grade Calculator computes the total average of all entered scores and determines the final letter grade based on this average. This final result not only helps students see their overall performance but also indicates whether they have passed the course. With this tool, students can easily monitor their progress and identify areas that may need improvement, ensuring they stay on track with their academic goals.
